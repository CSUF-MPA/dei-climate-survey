DEI Faculty Climate Survey

Your feedback is important to us. All responses are anonymous and will help improve our department's climate and culture.

General Climate & Belonging

1. I feel comfortable sharing my beliefs, perspectives, and opinions within the public administration (PA) department.
2. Faculty meetings and departmental discussions provide space for all voices to be heard.
3. I feel my contributions are valued by my department.
4. I am reluctant to bring up issues that concern me for fear of retaliation or negative evaluation within the PA department.
5. The department community is welcoming and supportive.

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Culture

1. The public administration department values diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging (DEIB).
2. Faculty achievements related to DEIB are recognized and rewarded in my department.
3. I am satisfied with the racial and ethnic diversity of faculty in the PA department.
4. I am satisfied with the gender diversity of faculty in the PA department.
5. I am satisfied with diversity in departmental leadership positions.
6. I have access to DEIB-related workshops or training.

Experiences of Discrimination & Exclusion

1. I feel confident intervening when I witness bias or discrimination.
2. I know where to report incidents of discrimination or harassment.
3. I have confidence that reports of discrimination or harassment will be taken seriously and investigated fairly.
4. In the past two years, I have experienced discrimination from faculty or staff within our department or exclusion from departmental activities because of my identity (race/ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, political beliefs, etc.).

Professional Support & Development

1. I have opportunities to collaborate on teaching or research with colleagues in my department.
2. The public administration department supports my professional development goals.
3. I feel supported balancing teaching, research, and service responsibilities within the PA department.
4. I feel my preferences are taken into consideration when making class scheduling decisions within my department.
5. Expectations around service and workload are fair and equitable within the department.

Open-Ended Questions

1. Do you have the opportunity to take on assigned time positions that you are interested in? Why or why not?
2. Do you have the opportunity to teach courses you are interested in? Why or why not?
3. Within the PA department, what current practices best support your sense of inclusion and belonging?
4. What is one action that could be taken within the PA department to improve faculty experience and/or foster community?

All responses are anonymous and confidential.
